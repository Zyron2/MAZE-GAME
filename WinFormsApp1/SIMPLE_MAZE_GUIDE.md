## ?? How to Play

### Start
- Position: Left side (green P)
- Goal: Reach right side (gold E)
- Avoid: Red monsters (M)

### Navigate
1. Use **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to move
2. Look for **clear paths** (white spaces)
3. Avoid **dark gray walls**
4. Stay away from **red monsters**

### Win
- Reach the **Exit (E)** on the right
- Game ends automatically
- Your time is recorded
