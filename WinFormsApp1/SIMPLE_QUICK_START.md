## ?? How to Play (30 seconds)

### Step 1: Start
- You're at the **LEFT** (Green "P")
- Goal is at the **RIGHT** (Gold "E")
- 2 monsters to avoid

### Step 2: Move
- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to move
- Walls are **dark gray** (easy to see!)
- Empty spaces are **white** (walk here!)

### Step 3: Avoid
- Monster 1: **Middle area** (stay above)
- Monster 2: **Right area** (stay below)
- Both are easy to avoid!

### Step 4: Win
- Reach the **EXIT (E)**
- Game ends!
- Your time is recorded!
